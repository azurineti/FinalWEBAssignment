Course topics: "Space in the heart from birth"

1. The Solar System

Learn about the Sun, planets, and their moons.

2. Constellations

Discover the stories behind popular star patterns.

3. Telescopes

Explore how telescopes work and their importance in astronomy.

4. Space Exploration

Learn about space missions and astronauts.

5. Earth's Moon

Understand the phases of the Moon and its features.

6. Space Travel

Discuss the possibilities of future space tourism and colonization.

7. Comets and Asteroids

Explore these celestial objects and their impact on Earth.

8. Black Holes

Introduce the concept of these mysterious, powerful objects in space.

9. Galaxies

Discuss different types of galaxies and their sizes.

10. Space Rocks

Learn about meteoroids, meteors, and meteorites.
